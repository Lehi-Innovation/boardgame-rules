Bluffing and prediction are the 
watchwords in this thrilling chase.
Will you be able to foil your 
opponent’s plans to win it all?
Embark on a dizzying manhunt 
to the 4 corners of the globe!
Play as Angela Farbank, a genius counterfeiter 
who travels the world cashing fake checks, 
or Jessah Hope, a relentless FBI agent 
who dreams of putting Farbank behind bars.

Round after round, Angela Farbank and Jessah Hope will battle it 
out to achieve their personal objective first and win the game.
Whatever your role, 4 random Action cards will be dealt 
each round to determine the choices available to you and 
your adversary. Place your tokens each turn to plan how 
and when to act, then resolve the actions according to the 
value of the played tokens.

Game Goal

1. Programming
Draw the top 4 Action cards and place them face up on the Action board, starting from the 
side of the Initiative token.
Randomly draw 3 of your Action tokens from those still available, without revealing them 
to your opponent.
Taking turns, starting with the Character with Initiative, place a token face down next to an 
Action card, on one of the two designated spaces.
Each space can only take one token. You cannot place two of your tokens on both sides of 
the same card.
When you have both placed your 3 tokens, move on to the next phase.

2. Action Resolution
Resolve the Action cards in order, starting with the one on the side of the Initiative token. 
Each time, reveal the tokens on each side of the card (don’t reveal the tokens on the other 
cards yet).
Depending on the configuration, there could be 0, 1, or 2 tokens assigned to each card.
 0 tokens: Ignore the card and move on to the next one.
 1 token: The owner of the token performs the card’s 2 Actions. They start with the 
Action on the side of the card with their token and then perform the other Action.
 2 tokens: Compare the values of the 2 tokens. The owner of the token with the 
highest value performs the card’s 2 Actions, starting with the Action next to their token. 
Once they’ve performed the 2 Actions, it’s their opponent’s turn, who can only perform 1 
Action: the one next to their token.

3. Preparation for the next round
Flip the Initiative token to its other side and place it on the corresponding Character’s side 
of the board. They now have Initiative.
Discard the 4 used Action cards in a face-up pile. You can look through them any time to 
check which of your opponent’s tokens are still available.
Begin a new round.
Every 3 rounds, when you have used all your 9 tokens and 12 Action cards, reset them. 
Mix up your tokens face down without looking at them, and make a new draw deck with 
the 12 shuffled Action cards.
