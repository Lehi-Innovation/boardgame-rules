# Solstis

## Overview

Solstis is a 2-player tile-capture game (with solo mode) in which players compete to build the most impressive mountain panorama. On each turn, you play a tile from your hand to capture matching tiles from a shared queue, placing everything into your personal panorama landscape. Forming 2×2 groups earns mystical Spirit tiles that modify scoring. The player with the most stars at the end wins.

## Components

- 48 Land tiles (each has a number value and a background color; some have fire icons)
- 12 Rainbow tiles
- 15 Spirit tiles (brown = immediate effect; blue = end-game scoring bonus)

## Setup

1. Shuffle all 48 Land tiles.
2. Create a central queue of 6 two-tile stacks: place the bottom tile face down, the top tile face up.
3. Each player draws 3 Land tiles as their starting hand.
4. Remaining Land tiles form a face-down draw pile.
5. Shuffle the 15 Spirit tiles into their own face-down stack; set aside a Spirit discard area.
6. Keep the 12 Rainbow tiles in a general supply.
7. Decide who goes first.

## Turn Structure

On your turn, perform these steps:

### 1. Play a Tile
Choose one Land tile from your hand and play it.

### 2. Check for Capture
Compare your played tile to the face-up tiles in the queue:
- If your tile's **number value** OR **background color** matches any face-up queue tile, you capture that tile.
- If multiple tiles could be captured, you choose exactly **one** to capture.
- The played tile and the captured tile are both added to your panorama.

### 3. If No Capture Is Possible
- Place your played tile face-up into the queue.
- Take 1 **Rainbow tile** from the supply and add it to your panorama.

### 4. Check for Spirit
If you completed at least one **2×2 group** of tiles in your panorama this turn, you earn one Spirit tile:
- Either take any face-up Spirit from the Spirit discard area, OR
- Draw 2 tiles from the Spirit stack face-down, choose 1 to keep, and place the unchosen tile face-up into the Spirit discard area.

Place the chosen Spirit face-up on the intersection of the four tiles forming the 2×2 group.

- **Brown Spirit:** Apply its effect immediately when placed.
- **Blue Spirit:** Provides an end-game scoring bonus (keep face-up in your panorama).

### 5. Draw a Tile
Draw 1 Land tile from the draw pile to return your hand to 3 tiles (if tiles remain).

## Rainbow Tiles

- Rainbow tiles fill spaces in your panorama to extend your contiguous area but **do not score points** on their own.
- **Moving a Rainbow:** If you later place a Land tile on a space occupied by a Rainbow (not covered by a Spirit), you may move that Rainbow tile to any other adjacent legal space in your panorama.

## End of Game

The game ends when the Land tile draw pile is depleted and both players have passed consecutively:
- After the draw pile is empty, players continue taking turns as long as they can capture.
- If you cannot capture on your turn, **pass**.
- When **both players pass consecutively**, the game ends.

**Final step:** Add all remaining tiles in your hand to your panorama. This placement does not trigger Spirit bonuses.

## Actions

### Capture
Play a tile from your hand that matches a face-up queue tile by number value or background color. Take the matching queue tile. Place both the played tile and the captured tile into your panorama. (You capture exactly one tile even if multiple matches exist.)

### Add to Queue (No Capture)
If your played tile matches nothing in the queue, place it face-up into the queue. Take 1 Rainbow tile from the supply and add it to your panorama.

### Place a Rainbow
Add a Rainbow tile to any legal space in your panorama that is adjacent to an existing tile. Rainbows extend your contiguous area but do not score. If a Land tile is later placed onto a Rainbow's space (not covered by a Spirit), move the Rainbow to any other adjacent legal space.

### Earn a Spirit
Triggered automatically if you complete a 2×2 group in your panorama on your turn. Choose: take any face-up Spirit from the Spirit discard area, or draw 2 from the Spirit stack face-down, keep 1, place the other face-up in the Spirit discard area. Place the chosen Spirit tile at the 2×2 group's intersection.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

Count your **stars**:

1. **Largest contiguous tile area:** Each Land tile in your largest connected group of tiles = **1 star**. Rainbow tiles do not score but may bridge/connect regions. Gaps or isolated tiles are not included.

2. **Fire icons:** Each Land tile with a fire icon that has a continuous path of tiles leading to the bottom row of your panorama = **1 additional star**.

3. **Spirit tiles:** Each Spirit tile in your panorama = **1 star**, plus any bonus stars from blue Spirit end-game effects.

The player with the **most stars** wins. (Tiebreaker not specified in available sources — consult the official rulebook.)

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **Queue management:** When a face-up tile in the queue is captured, the face-down tile beneath it becomes the new face-up tile in that stack position. When a stack is exhausted, that position in the queue remains empty.
- **Spirit placement:** A Spirit tile is placed on the corner intersection of the four tiles forming the 2×2 group; it covers that intersection point and belongs to your panorama.
- **Rainbow under a Spirit:** A Rainbow tile covered by a Spirit tile cannot be moved.
- **Multiple 2×2 groups in one turn:** You earn only **one** Spirit tile per turn regardless of how many 2×2 groups are completed.
- **Solo mode:** A solo mode is included in the rulebook (detailed rules not captured here — refer to the official rulebook for the solo opponent rules and target scores).

## Player Reference

| Situation | Result |
|---|---|
| Tile played matches queue tile (value or color) | Capture one matching tile; both go to your panorama |
| No capture possible | Played tile joins queue; take 1 Rainbow |
| 2×2 group completed this turn | Earn 1 Spirit tile |
| Brown Spirit | Immediate effect |
| Blue Spirit | End-game scoring bonus |
| Draw pile empty + both pass | Game ends; add hand to panorama |

**Scoring Summary:**
- 1 star per Land tile in largest contiguous area
- 1 star per fire-icon tile with path to bottom row
- 1 star per Spirit tile (+ blue Spirit bonuses)
