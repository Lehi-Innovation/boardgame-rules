## Overview

Red Notice is an asymmetric, hidden-movement bluffing game for exactly 2 players. One player is **Angela Farbank**, the **Counterfeiter**, who travels the world cashing fake cheques; the other is **Jessah Hope**, the **Federal Agent**, who hunts Angela down. Each player pursues their own objective, and the first to reach it wins:

- **Angela (Counterfeiter)** wins by cashing cheques worth a total of **$1,000,000 or more**.
- **Jessah (Federal Agent)** wins by **capturing Angela 3 times**: the first two captures each strip Angela of a False Identity; the third capture wins the game.

Every round, 4 random Action cards are dealt to a shared Action board. Players secretly assign numbered tokens to the sides of those cards to bid for the right to act, then resolve the cards in order. Angela records her position secretly behind a screen; Jessah tracks her on the shared World Map using Radars and Barriers.

## Components

**Shared:**
- 1 World Map board (Security track printed on the Counterfeiter's side, side B)
- 1 Action board
- 12 Action cards
- 18 Cheque cards (denominations of $100,000 and $200,000)
- 1 Initiative token

**Counterfeiter (Angela):**
- 1 Character piece
- 1 Personal board (printed on the back of the game box, used as a screen)
- 6 False Identity tiles (only 2 are used per game)
- 9 Action tokens
- 1 Security marker

**Federal Agent (Jessah):**
- 1 Character piece
- 10 Improvement cards
- 6 Radar tokens
- 4 Barrier tokens
- 9 Action tokens

Each player's 9 Action tokens include a special **POW!** token (see Actions).

## Setup

**Shared:**
1. Place the World Map board between the players, with the Security track on the Counterfeiter's side (side B).
2. Place the Action board above the World Map board.
3. Shuffle the 12 Action cards into a face-down deck next to the Action board.
4. Shuffle the 18 Cheque cards and deal **3 face down to each player**; place the remaining deck next to the World Map board.

**Federal Agent (Jessah):**
1. Mix your 9 Action tokens face down in front of you without looking at them.
2. Place your 4 Barrier tokens and 6 Radar tokens in a supply, **white side up**.
3. Shuffle the Improvement cards into a face-down deck on its space at the edge of the board and **reveal 2 Improvement cards** next to it.
4. Place your Character piece on the map in a city on a **continent matching the colour of one of your 3 Cheques**. Show that Cheque to your opponent, then place it on the bottom of the Cheque deck. Keep the other 2 Cheques as your starting hand.
5. Take 2 Radar tokens and place them on **2 continents of your choice**, white side up.

**Counterfeiter (Angela):**
1. Mix your 9 Action tokens face down without looking at them.
2. Randomly take **2 of your 6 False Identity tiles**, face up; put the other 4 away (unused this game). *(First game suggestion: Journalist and Pilot.)*
3. Place your Security marker on **space 2** of the Security track.
4. Stand the box up as a screen with the map on its back facing you (this is your personal board).
5. Behind the screen, place your Character piece on your personal board in a city on a **continent matching the colour of one of your 3 Cheques**. Show that Cheque to your opponent, then place it on the bottom of the Cheque deck. Jessah learns only your **starting continent**, not your exact city. Keep the other 2 Cheques as your starting hand.

**Initiative:** The Counterfeiter always has Initiative at the start. Place the Initiative token, Angela side showing, on her side of the Action board.

## Turn Structure

A game is played over a series of rounds until one player achieves their objective. Each round has 3 phases:

**1. Programming**
- Draw the top 4 Action cards and place them face up on the Action board, starting from the Initiative side.
- Each player randomly draws **3 of their still-available Action tokens** (without revealing them).
- Taking turns, starting with the player who has Initiative, place one token **face down** next to an Action card, on one of the card's two designated side-spaces. Each space holds only one token, and **you may not place two of your own tokens on both sides of the same card**.
- When both players have placed all 3 of their tokens, move to resolution.

**2. Action Resolution**
Resolve the Action cards one at a time, starting from the Initiative side. Reveal the tokens on both sides of the current card only (leave other cards' tokens hidden). For each card:
- **0 tokens:** Ignore the card.
- **1 token:** The token's owner performs **both** of the card's Actions, starting with the Action next to their token.
- **2 tokens:** Compare values. The higher-value token's owner performs **both** Actions (starting with the one next to their token); then the opponent performs **only 1** Action — the one next to their own token.
- **Tie (equal values):** Each player performs only the Action on their own side of the card. The player with Initiative resolves first.
- **POW! token:** Each player has one valueless **POW!** token. When revealed, it **cancels** the opponent's token on the other side of the card, letting the POW! player perform **both** of the card's Actions as if only one token were placed. If both players play POW! on the same card, it is a draw (resolve as a tie).

All Actions are optional — if you must perform an Action but cannot or do not want to, ignore it. Finish a card completely before moving to the next.

**3. Preparation for the Next Round**
- Flip the Initiative token to its other side and place it on that character's side — they now have Initiative.
- Discard the 4 used Action cards to a face-up pile (either player may inspect it any time to see which tokens the opponent has already used).
- **Every 3 rounds** (once all 9 tokens and all 12 Action cards have been used), reset: reshuffle your tokens face down without looking, and rebuild the Action deck from the 12 shuffled cards.

## Actions

There are **4 Action types** — Cheque, Bank, Security, Movement — plus a **Wild** Action that lets you perform any one of the 4. A card shows Actions with symbols (1 or 2 of a symbol).

**Cheque** — For each Cheque symbol, draw a Cheque card from the deck and secretly add it to your hand. You may **never hold more than 5 Cheques**; if your hand is full, do not draw.

**Bank (cash a Cheque)** — Cash **one (and only one)** Cheque from your hand. You must be in a city of the **same colour** as the Cheque. Place it face up. On cashing, apply the Cheque's value effect (below). Cashing **exposes your location** — you must state the city you are in.

*Cheque values and effects (apply to whoever cashes):*
- **$100,000:** You may immediately perform 1 Movement (standard Movement rules).
- **$200,000:** Jessah gains an Improvement (see Special Rules).

**Security** — This Action differs by role:
- *Jessah:* For each Security symbol (1 or 2), place **1 Barrier OR 1 Radar** on the World Map (white side up).
  - **Barrier:** placed on a road between two cities; Angela can never use that road. Max 1 Barrier per road; only 4 exist. A Security Action can also be used to **move** a Barrier from one road to another.
  - **Radar:** placed on one of the 6 Radar spaces (one per continent); tracks Angela's movement on that continent. Max 1 Radar per space.
- *Angela:* Each Security symbol advances your **Security marker** 1 space (start at 2, **max 4** — excess is lost). At **any time** you may spend **2 Security points** to remove a Barrier or Radar from the map, returning it to supply. You may only remove **white-side** tokens, never black-side. You may spend 2 points just before gaining more so as not to lose them.

**Movement** — Each Movement symbol (1 or 2) lets you move from your city to an adjacent city along a connecting road. (Note: **Peking is linked to Los Angeles**.) You may waive some or all of your Movements. Role-specific rules:
- *Jessah:* Each time you **enter a new city, you search it** — Angela must tell you if she is there. If she is not, continue normally. If she **is**, you **capture** her (this ends your current Action). If Angela still has at least 1 Identity, she must discard one (her choice) to escape, immediately applying its effect, then flipping it face down (that Identity is unmasked and can no longer be used). To search the city you are already in before leaving it, you must spend a Movement.
- *Angela:* Track your movement on your hidden personal board. Your movement is constrained by Jessah's security:
  - **Barriers:** you can never use a road blocked by a Barrier.
  - **Radars:** if you are in a city on a continent under (white-side) Radar surveillance, you **trigger and deactivate** it. At the end of your Movement, tell Jessah **all** Radars you triggered and in what order; they return to supply (except black-side ones). You need not reveal which cities you passed through or when. Passing through several cities on one surveilled continent triggers its Radar **only once**.

*You do NOT trigger a Radar if:* you are only leaving a continent (a Radar triggers only on **entering** a city on its continent); Jessah places a Radar on a continent where you already are (it triggers only if you then move to another city there); or you don't use your Movements and stay put. You may use a Movement to trigger and deactivate a Radar without changing city.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

The game ends immediately when either player achieves their objective:
- **Angela (Counterfeiter) wins** the instant her cashed Cheques total **$1,000,000 or more**.
- **Jessah (Federal Agent) wins** by **capturing Angela a 3rd time**, after having removed both of Angela's False Identities.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

**False Identities (Angela's escapes).** Angela starts with 2 of her 6 Identity tiles in play. Each of Jessah's first two captures forces Angela to discard one Identity (her choice), immediately apply its effect, and flip it face down (unusable thereafter). Unless a tile says otherwise, Movements from an Identity effect obey the normal Barrier/Radar rules, and Angela may spend Security points while resolving an Identity. The six tiles:
- **Lawyer:** Discard 1 Barrier and 1 Radar (white side showing), then perform up to 2 Movements.
- **Journalist:** Perform up to 2 Movements; you may move through Barriers and do **not** trigger/deactivate Radars (regardless of colour), and need not tell Jessah if you passed through a Barrier.
- **Secret Agent:** Perform up to 2 Movements, then set your Security marker to 4.
- **Doctor:** Draw 2 Cheques, then perform up to 2 Movements.
- **Businesswoman:** Destroy one Permanent Improvement in front of Jessah (its effects are cancelled), then perform up to 2 Movements. If it is a Permanent *Unit* Improvement, Angela chooses which Radar and Barrier are removed from the board.
- **Pilot:** Perform up to 3 Movements.

**Improvement cards (Jessah's rewards).** Any time **either** character cashes a **$200,000 Cheque**, Jessah gains 1 of the 2 face-up Improvement cards — **the character who cashed the Cheque chooses which** of the two Jessah takes; the other is permanently discarded. Jessah uses it immediately: **Permanent Improvements** stay in front of her; **Immediate-effect Improvements** are discarded after use. Reveal 2 new Improvements afterward. Once the Improvement deck is empty, no more are revealed, and from then on every $200,000 Cheque cashed instead grants Jessah **1 Security Action followed by 1 Movement Action** (as shown on the board).

**Black-side tokens.** Radars and Barriers normally sit white side up and return to supply when removed/triggered. Certain Improvements flip a token to its **black side**, making it persistent — Angela cannot remove black-side tokens with Security points, and a black-side Radar is not returned to supply when triggered.

**Hidden information.** Jessah knows only Angela's starting continent, never her exact city, until Angela reveals it by cashing a Cheque (which forces her to state her city) or is found during a search. Angela need not reveal whether she used her Movements or which route she took.

**Cheque hand limit.** You may never hold more than 5 Cheques; forced draws over the limit are lost.

## Player Reference

**Roles & win conditions**
- Angela (Counterfeiter): cash **$1,000,000+** in Cheques.
- Jessah (Federal Agent): **capture Angela 3×** (1st & 2nd remove an Identity, 3rd wins).

**Round:** 1) Programming (deal 4 Action cards; each secretly places 3 of their tokens) → 2) Resolution (reveal & resolve card-by-card from Initiative side) → 3) Preparation (pass Initiative, discard cards; reset tokens & deck every 3 rounds).

**Token resolution per card:** 0 = skip · 1 = owner does both Actions · 2 = higher does both, lower does 1 · tie = each does own side (Initiative first) · POW! = cancels opponent, do both.

**Four Actions (+ Wild = any one):**
- **Cheque:** draw 1 Cheque per symbol (hand max 5).
- **Bank:** cash 1 Cheque (must match city colour; states your city). $100k → 1 free Movement; $200k → Jessah gains Improvement.
- **Security:** Jessah places Barrier/Radar (per symbol); Angela advances Security marker (max 4; spend 2 to remove a white-side token).
- **Movement:** move 1 city per symbol along roads (Peking↔Los Angeles). Jessah searches each new city; Angela triggers Radars and is blocked by Barriers.

**Security marker:** starts at 2, max 4; 2 points removes one white-side Radar/Barrier.

**Supplies:** Jessah has 6 Radars (6 continents) and 4 Barriers (roads).
