## Overview

Comanauts is a cooperative adventure game set in the mind of Dr. Martin Strobal, a brilliant scientist in a coma. Players are "comanauts" who enter his subconscious, navigating through bizarre dream spaces to find clues and defeat the inner demons (I.D.s) troubling him. The game uses a unique storybook that serves as rules reference, story guide, and game board all in one. It can be played as individual sessions or a campaign.

## Components

- 1 Rulebook
- 1 Adventure Book (serves as both rules reference and game map)
- 1 Sideboard (with Group Task Track, Inner Child Slot, Threat Track, Enemy Slots, Morass, Discard Pool, Insight Token Slot, Situation Token Slot)
- 1 Dice Bag with 38 Dice (attribute dice in colors: red, green, yellow, blue, purple; white clarity dice; black threat dice; 1 transparent blue inner child die)
- 60 Item Cards
- 11 Totem Cards
- 6 Environment Cards
- 22 Avatar Cards and standees
- 32 Hostile Cards and standees
- 13 I.D. Entity Cards and standees
- 55 Clue Cards
- 11 Comazone Cards
- 10 Vital Signs Cards (including 1 Flatline card and Stable/Critical cards)
- 24 Status Cards
- 4 Rules Reference Cards, 4 Player Sheets
- Tokens: Clarity, Insight, Fire, Search, Life Force, Bookmark, Door, Suspicious, Objective, Situation

## Setup

1. Give each player a Player Sheet and their default Status Card (face down in the slot).
2. Place all dice in the Dice Bag.
3. Form the Vital Signs Deck: shuffle the Flatline card with 2 Stable vital signs cards face down; shuffle the rest of the vital signs cards and place them face down on top of those 3.
4. Set aside separate piles of item, hostile, I.D. Entity, avatar, totem, and status cards (no need to shuffle).
5. Remove 2 copies of the Fear I.D. Entity. (In campaign, also remove any I.D. Entities with stickers.) Shuffle the remaining I.D. Entities and reveal 5; return the rest to the box.
6. Collect the Comazone Cards for each revealed I.D. Entity and lay them in a face-up row (the Nexus) next to the Adventure Book. Randomly choose one to be the starting comazone and turn to its page.
7. Collect totem cards for each comazone in the Nexus to form the Totem Deck.
8. Set aside the I.D. Entity for the starting comazone. Shuffle the remaining revealed I.D. Entities and draw 1 without looking — this is the **Prime I.D. Entity**. Locate its 7-digit code, collect and shuffle its 5 matching Clue Cards, and place them face down on top of the Prime I.D. Entity card. Place all other I.D. Entities in a separate face-down I.D. Stack.
9. Deal 2 random avatar cards face down onto each player's avatar slot. Each player then chooses a starting avatar from the remaining cards, places it on top, and adds 3 Life Force tokens to it (plus any bonus). Return unused avatar cards. Each player collects her starting standee and starting items.
10. Choose a first Bookkeeper (give that player the Bookmark token). Place the Sideboard next to the Adventure Book with the Situation Token "safe" side up. Read the introduction of the starting comazone.

## Turn Structure

On each player's turn (the Bookkeeper goes first; play proceeds clockwise):

1. **Draw Dice**: Draw 5 dice from the Dice Bag. If fewer than 5 remain, draw what's left, move all dice from the Discard Pool back to the bag, then complete the draw.
2. **Gain Clarity**: Roll white (clarity) dice individually or together. After each roll, if the result equals or exceeds your current number of Clarity tokens, gain 1 Clarity token and discard that die to the Discard Pool.
3. **Place Threat**: Place all black dice with white pips (common threat dice) on the Threat Track from the top down. If your avatar has a Suspicious token, also place black dice with red pips (suspicious threat dice); otherwise return them to the bag.
   - If the situation is "hostile" and there are more threat dice than enemies in play, return a previously defeated hostile from this page to the map.
4. **Place Inner Child**: If the transparent blue die was drawn, roll it, place it on the Inner Child Slot on the Sideboard, and reveal a Vital Signs Card. If the map page has a Rabbit Hole symbol, place the Inner Child standee on that space.
5. **Perform Actions**: Spend remaining colored (attribute) dice to perform actions. To spend a die: roll it, resolve its effect, discard it to the Discard Pool. You may perform as many actions as you like in any order, as long as you have dice. (See Actions section.)
6. **Discard Dice**: Discard all remaining dice.
7. **Check Threat**:
   - If the situation is "hostile": when the number of threat dice on the track equals or exceeds the number of enemies in play, the enemies take a turn (Enemy Turn).
   - If the situation is "safe": when there are 4 or more dice on the threat track, resolve the current page's designated effect (per the Adventure Book); if enemies come into play, flip the Situation Token to "hostile" and take an Enemy Turn. Then discard all dice from the track and refresh the Dice Bag.
   - After Check Threat, the next player (to the left) takes their turn.

## Actions

All actions require spending an attribute die (roll it, resolve effect, discard). Purple dice can substitute for any color but receive no color-specific bonuses.

| Action | Die Required | Effect |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **Focus** | Any 2 attribute dice | Take 1 attribute die of your choice from the Discard Pool |
| **Move** | Any attribute die | Move avatar across a dotted line to an adjacent space, or within current space. Use a matching color die to cross a solid colored line |
| **Reserve** | Any attribute die | Store 1 die on your Player Sheet (no rolling needed) for later use or defense. Limit: 1 reserved die at a time |
| **Encourage** | Any attribute die | Store 1 die on another player's sheet, OR discard a colored die to give that player 1 of your Clarity tokens |
| **Equip/Trade** | Any attribute die | Move items between inventory and equipment slots, or exchange items with a player in the same space |
| **Skill Test** | Matching color dice | Roll to meet or exceed a target number; success resolves the success effect |
| **Group Task** | Matching color dice | Place dice on the Group Task Track; when total meets target, resolve the success effect (no failure possible) |
| **Melee Attack** | Red dice | Target an enemy in the same space; if roll ≥ enemy defense, enemy is defeated and looted |
| **Ranged Attack** | Green dice | Target an enemy in same or adjacent space (farther with a ranged weapon), within clear sight |
| **Search** | Yellow dice | On a space with a Search token; roll 4+ to succeed, gain 1 item from the Search entry, discard 1 Search token |

### Enemy Turn
When enemies take a turn, activate each enemy in slot order:
1. Take a die from the Threat Track and roll it; match the result to the enemy's card action.
2. Move the enemy toward the nearest avatar until it enters that space or exhausts its speed.
3. Target the closest avatar within range and clear sight.
4. Attack: if the target has no reserved die (or chooses not to defend), remove 1 Life Force from that avatar. If the target defends with a reserved die: roll it — if roll ≥ attack strength, the attack is blocked (die returned to Player Sheet); otherwise, discard the die and still remove 1 Life Force.
After all enemies activate, discard remaining threat dice and refresh the Dice Bag.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

### Win
Players win if they find and defeat the **Prime I.D. Entity**. When the Prime I.D. Entity is encountered and added to play, place Life Force tokens on its card equal to the number of players. To defeat the Prime I.D., attack it repeatedly: each time it would be defeated, remove 1 Life Force token from its card instead. When it has no Life Force tokens, it is defeated.

### Lose (any one condition)
- The **Flatline** Vital Signs Card is revealed.
- Any player's **third avatar** is sent to the Morass.
- **All avatars** are in the Morass at the same time.

### Finding the Prime I.D.
Reveal Clue Cards by collecting 3 Insight tokens (discard 3 Insight tokens → draw and read the top Clue Card). Clues hint at the emotional theme of the comazone where the Prime I.D. resides. The correct comazone is never the starting comazone, and any I.D. Entity already encountered is not the Prime I.D. Players navigate the Nexus (choosing which comazones to visit) until they locate and defeat the Prime I.D.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- **The Morass**: When an avatar loses all its Life Force tokens, it ceases to exist in the current comazone. Reveal a Vital Signs Card, remove the avatar from play, discard its avatar card and unused dice, and flip to the next avatar card in the player's stack (add 3 Life Force tokens to the new avatar). The new avatar's standee is placed in the Morass section of the Sideboard. At the beginning of that player's next turn, the avatar returns to play on any space occupied by another avatar. The game is lost if any player's third avatar enters the Morass, or if all avatars are in the Morass simultaneously.
- **Vital Signs Cards**: Revealed when an avatar enters the Morass, when the inner child die is drawn, or when the Adventure Book directs. Stable cards have no effect. Critical cards may trigger the comazone's Critical entry (a second Critical card on the same page has no effect). The Flatline card causes immediate defeat.
- **Clarity Tokens**: Spent to re-roll a die (1 token per re-roll, no limit per die), or to activate an avatar's unique Clarity Ability (cost listed on the avatar card, usually 3 tokens).
- **Suspicious Tokens**: An avatar in a comazone listed as suspicious on its avatar card carries a Suspicious Token. Suspicious tokens cause suspicious threat dice (black with red pips) to be placed on the Threat Track, making the threat more dangerous.
- **Totems**: Gained alongside Clue Cards. Totems function as items and can be used to travel between comazones. Must be equipped to use.
- **Status Cards**: Avatars can be affected by conditions (e.g., Trapped, On Fire). Effect applies immediately; follow the "Removal" instruction on the card to remove it. An avatar cannot carry two copies of the same status card.
- **Leaving a Page**: Unless stated otherwise, finish the current player's turn (including Check Threat) before leaving. Remove all tokens and enemies from that page; move all avatars to their cards.
- **The Nexus**: A row of comazone cards set out during setup. When the Adventure Book sends players to the Nexus, each player places her avatar on its card and the group chooses the next comazone to visit.
- **Campaign Play**: Each session defeats one I.D. By defeating multiple I.D.s across sessions, players unlock more story and can eventually wake Dr. Strobal.

## Player Reference

**Turn Order (each player in sequence):**
1. Draw Dice (5 from bag)
2. Gain Clarity (roll white dice)
3. Place Threat (black dice → Threat Track)
4. Place Inner Child (if blue die drawn, reveal Vital Signs Card)
5. Perform Actions (spend attribute dice)
6. Discard Dice
7. Check Threat (trigger Enemy Turn if threshold met)

**Lose if:** Flatline Vital Signs card revealed / any player's 3rd avatar enters Morass / all avatars in Morass simultaneously
**Win if:** Prime I.D. Entity defeated (place Life Force tokens on its card equal to the number of players when encountered; defeated once all Life Force tokens are removed from it)

**Insight → Clues:** Every 3 Insight tokens collected (discard 3) → reveal 1 Clue Card
