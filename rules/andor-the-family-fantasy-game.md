## Overview

Andor: The Family Fantasy Game is a cooperative adventure game where players work together as heroes (magician, warrior, archer, and dwarf) to complete quests. Players explore a single fixed game board, find wolf cubs in the dwarven mine, and overcome challenges together. The game features progressive difficulty across multiple games, with different task card combinations introducing new challenges. All players win or lose together.

## Components

- 1 Game board (fixed)
- 4 Hero figures (Magician, Warrior, Archer, Dwarf)
- 4 Hero boards (each double-sided, showing a hero on one side and a heroine on the other)
- Wolf cub tokens
- Monster tokens
- Fog tokens
- Task cards
- Sun discs (personal tokens for each hero)
- Dice

## Setup

Setup varies by adventure. For the introductory adventure:
1. Place the single game board in the middle of the table.
2. Place hero figures at the starting position (Rietburg, upper left corner).
3. Place fog tokens on unexplored spaces to the left of the river.
4. Each hero takes their personal sun discs and places them on their hero board.

## Turn Structure

The player holding the night marker goes first. After that, play proceeds clockwise. On your turn:

1. **Move or Stay Still:** Move your hero step by step across the board, or stay still. Each step (and staying still) costs the active hero one of their own sun discs. When a hero runs out of sun discs, they go to sleep.
2. **Carry Out Actions:** Perform actions at your target space (fight monsters, pick up items, reveal fog tokens, etc.). Actions do not cost sun discs.

Once all heroes are out of sun discs and asleep, night falls. During the night, creatures move, then each hero's sun discs are restored and a new day begins.

## Actions

### Movement

Move your hero step by step along connected spaces. Each step costs one sun disc. You cannot enter the river — you may only cross it via the bridge. Staying still also costs one sun disc.

### Combat

When a hero ends their move on a space with a Gor, they may fight it. Combat requires rolling sword symbols to fill a fixed number of sword storage spaces around the Gor: 2 swords are needed in forest spaces, and 3 swords are needed in mountain spaces. A hero rolls all their dice once per turn and places dice showing swords on the storage spaces. The battle can be continued over multiple turns (as long as sun discs remain), and other heroes can assist by moving to the same space. Sword tokens from a loot bag each count as one rolled sword symbol. When all storage spaces are filled, the Gor is driven away and the dragon moves one space back on the dragon bar. There is no monster strength value to compare against.

### Special Abilities

Each hero has a unique feature:
- **Warrior:** Has 4 blue dice, giving more sword-rolling power in combat.
- **Archer:** Has 9 sun discs instead of 6, allowing more actions per day.
- **Magician:** Has one purple die showing flash symbols; rolling a flash immediately wins any battle against a Gor, regardless of free sword storage spaces.
- **Dwarf:** Can take shortcuts through caves — entering one cave and emerging from any other cave exit costs only one sun disc.

### Exploration

Reveal fog tokens when your move ends on a space with one. Fog may reveal items (wood, gold, swords), allies (Reka the herb witch), dragon movement, or new Gors.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

This is a cooperative game. All players win if they find all 3 wolf cubs in the dwarven mine before the dragon reaches Rietburg. All players lose immediately if the dragon reaches Rietburg.

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Heroes can share a space and help each other in combat.
- The game includes multiple adventures of increasing difficulty, achieved by using different numbered task card combinations across games.
- Each adventure has specific setup and victory conditions defined by its task cards.
- The bridge guard blocks the bridge until all task cards for the current game have been completed (turned to their green side).

## Player Reference

**Turn:** Move/Stay Still (costs hero's own sun discs) → Carry out actions at target space (free)

**Heroes:** Warrior (more dice in combat), Archer (9 sun discs), Magician (flash wins battles instantly), Dwarf (cave shortcuts)

**Win:** Find all 3 wolf cubs in the dwarven mine before the dragon reaches Rietburg.

**Lose:** The dragon reaches Rietburg.
