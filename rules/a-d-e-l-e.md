## Overview

A semi-cooperative space horror game where crew members try to survive a rogue AI aboard a spacecraft bound for Mars. One player controls ADELE (Advanced Device of Electronic-Living Engineering), the hostile AI, while the remaining players are crew members who must complete one of two missions: disconnect ADELE or escape the ship. Both missions require finding specific objects and delivering them to specific locations. If time runs out or a crew member dies, ADELE wins.

## Components

- 1 Main Board (double-sided: Deimos/Phobos)
- 1 ADELE Console
- 4 Crew Boards and 4 Crew Screens
- 27 ADELE cards
- 25 Event cards
- 19 Mission tiles
- 32 Object tiles
- 6 Anomaly tiles
- 28 Cubes (7 each of 4 colors)
- 4 Astronaut meeples
- 52 Hazard tokens
- 16 Charge tokens
- 6 Hatch Closed tokens
- 2 Alert Terminal tokens
- 4 Impact tokens
- 8 Inventory Disabled tokens
- 1 Turn marker, 1 "No Return" marker
- 1d20 and 4d6
- 1 Cloth bag
- 2 Player aids

## Setup

1. Place main board centrally (choose Deimos or Phobos side).
2. Choose ADELE player. They get the console (matching player count side), all hazard tokens placed into the bag (ADELE draws 4 from the bag and places them on matching spaces on the console), ADELE deck (4 cards drawn), and energy counter.
3. ADELE selects 4 random anomaly tiles for console.
4. Other players take crew boards, screens, dice, and cubes. Place 1 cube on life track, 4 on action zone.
5. Place 5 specific objects (fire extinguisher, space suit, flashlight, battery, and tracking drone) face-down on the designated box printed on the ship. Then shuffle 20 specific objects (7 mission objects, 2 copies of object parts, 1 battery object, and 10 randomly selected objects) face-down and place one in each ship location.
6. Roll d20 for each crew member's starting location (no two in same space).
7. Place Alert Terminals on computers in locations 3 and 17.
8. Set up Mission Track with sector mission tiles and assign cubes.
9. Place turn marker based on player count. Place "No Return" marker on space 1.
10. Prepare Event Deck (remove Silence cards in 4-5 player games).

## Turn Structure

Each round has phases for crew actions and ADELE actions, with events triggering between them. Crew members use cubes as actions to move, search, interact with objects, and complete mission objectives. ADELE plays cards to place hazard tokens on ship locations and can activate anomalies (which may close hatches or produce other disruptive effects) by discarding hazards and paying energy.

## Actions

### Crew Actions

Each crew member has 4 action cubes. All actions except the Special Action cost 1 action cube. The five allowed actions are:

- **Advance**: Move to an adjacent location, as long as the door connecting to it is not blocked by a Blocked Door hazard.
- **Search**: Look at a face-down object in your location; you may move it into your inventory (which may force you to discard another object).
- **Use Object**: Flip a non-mission object you are carrying face-up to activate its effect.
- **Unlock Door**: Discard a Blocked Door hazard token from a door at your location.
- **Special Action**: Costs 3 action cubes (or 2 with a successful Spirit roll). Used to deliver a mission object if you are in the correct location, activate the effect of a terminal, discard certain hazard tokens, unlock a hatch blocked by the "Hatches Closed!" anomaly, or remove an Impact token from an adjacent location while in a space section.

Information sharing between crew members (including secretly viewing each other's sector mission tiles and exchanging objects) happens when two crew members are in the same location and is not a separate action.

### ADELE Actions
- **Play ADELE Cards**: Place hazard tokens on ship locations shown on the card by paying the required energy.
- **Deploy Hazard Tokens**: Place tokens drawn from the bag onto console spaces; when played to the board, always use the most expensive (rightmost) token of that type.
- **Activate Anomalies**: Discard 1 hazard from each required sector and pay 5 energy to flip an anomaly tile active, triggering its special effect.
- **Surveillance**: Monitor crew positions and objects via Espionage hazard tokens.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

**Crew wins** by completing either mission:
- **Disconnect ADELE**: Deliver 4 required objects to their specific ship locations; when the final object (the Axe) is delivered, there must be no impact token anywhere on the board.
- **Escape the Ship**: Deliver 4 required objects to their specific ship locations; when the final object (the Helmet) is delivered, the turn marker must not be beyond the "No Return" marker (it may be on the same space).

**ADELE wins** if:
- Time runs out (turn marker reaches end)
- Any crew member's life cube reaches the last space on their 6-space life track

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Mission knowledge is fragmented among crew members; they must coordinate while hiding information from ADELE.
- The "No Return" marker advances through the game, creating a point of no return for the escape mission.
- Hazard tokens drawn from the bag create unpredictable dangers.
- In 1-2 player games, use special solo/duo rules (section 11.0 of rulebook).
- Experienced players remove any 2 other special cards from ADELE's deck instead of the designated 2 extra special cards.
- Crew screens hide each player's planned actions and knowledge from the ADELE player.
- Event cards flip each round, creating ongoing environmental effects.

## Player Reference

### Crew Action Summary
- Actions = cubes in action zone (typically 4)
- Spend cubes to take actions
- Wounds reduce carrying capacity (inventory spaces), not action cubes

### Win Conditions
| Side | Condition |
|------|-----------|
| Crew | Complete disconnect OR escape mission |
| ADELE | Time runs out OR any crew member's life cube reaches the last space on their life track |
