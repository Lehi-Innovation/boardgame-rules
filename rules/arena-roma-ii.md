## Overview

Arena: Roma II is a 2-player card and dice game set during a revolt in Rome. Players compete for supremacy by playing character and building cards next to an icon bar, activating them with dice to earn victory points, collect money, attack opponents, and gain advantages. The player with the most victory points when the game ends wins.

## Components

- 55 cards: 26 character cards (yellow) and 29 building cards (green)
- 1 icon bar (5 parts, showing die results 1-6 plus bribery, money, and cards icons)
- 36 victory point tokens (1 VP and 2 VP pieces)
- Money (Sestertii) in various denominations
- 3 action dice per player, each player using their own color
- 1 white battle die
- Rules leaflet

## Setup

1. Assemble the icon bar and place centrally.
2. Each player takes 10 VP as personal supply and 3 action dice of their color.
3. Place remaining 16 VP, money, and battle die within reach.
4. Shuffle cards, deal 5 to each player. Each player gives 2 cards face down to their opponent.
5. Youngest player goes first: play all hand cards face up on their side of the icon bar, next to any die icon (1-6) or the bribery icon. Max 1 card per icon per player.
6. Other player does the same on their side.

## Turn Structure

Each turn follows this sequence:

1. **Evaluate vacant spaces (Phase 1)** -- For each unoccupied space on your side of the icon bar, forfeit 1 VP from your personal supply to the general supply. (The money and cards icons are never counted as vacant, as no cards can be played there.)
2. **Roll action dice (Phase 2)** -- Roll your 3 action dice. If all three show the same result, you may re-roll all three.
3. **Perform actions (Phase 3)** -- Assign your dice to actions and perform them in any order you choose.

## Actions

**Activate a Card:** Place a die on the die icon matching that die's number (or on the bribery icon). Execute the card's effect. Each die result may only be assigned to one action. Multiple dice may be assigned to the same non-bribery icon in a single turn.

**Collect Money:** Place a die on the money icon to collect that many Sestertii from the general supply.

**Draw Cards:** Place a die on the cards icon to draw that many cards from the face-down draw pile. Keep exactly 1 card; place all others face up on the discard pile.

**Bribery:** Place a die on the bribery icon to activate the card next to it. You must additionally pay Sestertii equal to the value of the die used. The bribery icon may only be used once per turn.

**Playing New Cards:** Does not require a die. Pay the card's cost in Sestertii to play it face up next to any icon on your side. If a card already occupies that space, discard the original card first.

**Battle:** Some cards allow attacking an opponent's card. Roll the white battle die. If the result equals or exceeds the target's defense value, the target is discarded. If lower, nothing happens.

## Scoring / Victory Conditions

- Players start with 10 VP each in their personal supply.
- VP are gained through card abilities and lost by forfeiting for vacant spaces or through opponent attacks.
- The game ends immediately when either:
  - Any player's personal VP supply is exhausted, **or**
  - The general VP supply is exhausted.
- The player with the most VP wins. (If the general supply runs out before a player can receive their last VP, those missing VP are still counted virtually.)

## Special Rules & Edge Cases

- Cards marked with "no die needed" are active as long as they are face up, requiring no die to use.
- Cards requiring 2 dice need 1 die to activate and 1 die to determine the effect strength.
- Character cards (yellow) are generally more aggressive; building cards (green) provide ongoing benefits.
- If a card is discarded through battle, the icon space it occupied becomes available.
- The draw pile can run out; if so, shuffle the discard pile to form a new draw pile.
- The **Speculator** card lets you look at your opponent's hand and take one of their cards into your hand.
- Arena: Roma II can be combined with the original Roma game for expanded options.

## Player Reference

| Die Use | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Match card number | Activate that card's ability |
| Money icon | Collect Sestertii equal to die value |
| Cards icon | Draw cards equal to die value; keep 1, discard rest |
| Bribery icon | Activate bribery card; pay Sestertii equal to die value (once per turn only) |

| Card Type | Color | Nature |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Character | Yellow | Aggressive/tactical |
| Building | Green | Economy/ongoing benefits |
