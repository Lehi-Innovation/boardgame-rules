### MAJOR FINDING 1: US Assault Rate in Highland — Missing "no US Base" Qualifier

**Summary (Actions table):** "ARVN: 1 per 2 cubes (1 per 3 in Highland). Remove NVA Troops first, then Active Guerrillas, then Bases (only after no Guerrillas remain). Underground Guerrillas immune. Tunneled Bases require die roll (4-6 removes Tunnel only)."

**Summary (Assault row):** "US: 2 enemies per Troop at US Base, 1 per Troop normally, **1 per 2 in Highland**."

**Source (3.2.4):** "Remove 2 enemies per US Troops cube if the space has a US Base, or only **1 enemy for every 2 US Troops (round down) if Highland with no US Base**, or otherwise 1 enemy piece for each US Troop."

**Error:** The summary omits the "no US Base" qualifier on the Highland reduced rate. In a Highland space WITH a US Base, the correct rate is 2 per US Troop (the higher rate), not 1 per 2. A player using the summary in a Highland space with a US Base would apply the wrong (lower) rate, removing far fewer enemy pieces than the rules allow.

Would mislead players at the table? **Yes** — directly affects how many enemy pieces are removed in a common scenario (US Base + Highland province).

### MAJOR FINDING 2: Bombard — NVA Troop Location Condition Misstated

**Summary:** "**Bombard**: In 1-2 spaces with 3+ ARVN/US Troops or a Base, **adjacent to 3+ NVA Troops**: remove 1 US/ARVN Troop."

**Source (4.4.2):** "It may occur in 1 or 2 spaces with any combination of at least 3 ARVN and/or US Troops (Police and Special Forces do not count) or with any US or ARVN Base. Each space also must have **in it or be adjacent to** a space with at least 3 NVA Troops."

**Error:** The summary says "adjacent to 3+ NVA Troops" but the source says the NVA Troops can be **in the target space itself or adjacent**. A player reading the summary would incorrectly believe Bombard cannot fire into a space that already contains 3+ NVA Troops (only spaces adjacent to such a space), missing the co-location case entirely.

Would mislead players at the table? **Yes** — a player using the summary would deny NVA the Bombard action in a common scenario (NVA Troops co-located with enemy forces).

- The summary omits the requirement that ARVN must outnumber US cubes to use the Aid→Patronage transfer option.

**Bombard — Police/SF exclusion omitted:**
- Source: "at least 3 ARVN and/or US Troops (Police and Special Forces do not count)"
