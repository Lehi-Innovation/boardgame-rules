# Audit Findings — Batch b030

Audited 2026-06-11. Game: `rules/battletech.md` compared against `extracted/battletech-rules.txt` (BattleTech: A Game of Armored Combat, 2018 box set rulebook).

---

## battletech — MAJOR

**Verdict: MAJOR** — The phase/movement skeleton, GATOR system, and critical-hit rules are largely faithful, but the summary contains an invented initiative mechanic that inverts the actual turn order, a wrong ammunition-explosion heat threshold with an inverted die roll, a wrong Death From Above damage formula, and a wrong standing-up MP cost. Any of these would mislead players at the table.

### Major findings

1. **Invented mechanic — initiative winner "chooses to move first or second."**
   - Summary (Initiative Phase): "The initiative winner chooses to move first or second (moving second is usually advantageous)."
   - Source (l.591-592): "MOVEMENT PHASE — The side that lost Initiative must act first."
   - There is no choice anywhere in the source; the loser always acts first. The parenthetical advice is also invented.

2. **Contradiction — firing order in the Weapon Attack Phase.**
   - Summary: "The initiative winner fires first."
   - Source (l.604-605): "WEAPON ATTACK PHASE — The side that lost Initiative must act first. They choose one of their ’Mechs to declare fire first"; and for resolution (l.627-628): "Again, the side that lost Initiative must act first."
   - The summary states the exact opposite of the source for both declaration and resolution.

3. **Wrong number + inverted roll — ammunition explosion from heat.**
   - Summary (Heat Management table): "13 | Possible ammunition explosion (roll 2d6, explodes on 4+)".
   - Source (l.3239-3244): "If a ’Mech with unemptied bins of ammunition reaches 19 points or higher on the Heat Scale, one of these bins may explode. To avoid an explosion, the ’Mech’s controller must roll 2D6 once and meet or exceed... a ’Mech with 19-22 heat would need to roll a 4 or higher, 23-27 heat a 6 or higher, and 28-30 heat an 8 or higher to avoid."
   - The threshold is 19+, not 13, and rolling 4+ *avoids* the explosion at 19-22 heat — the summary says 4+ *causes* it. As written, a player at heat 13 would be checking for explosions that cannot happen, and would explode on the rolls that should save them.

4. **Wrong formula — Death From Above damage.**
   - Summary (Physical Attacks table): "Death From Above | Jump onto target | Weight / 5 per leg".
   - Source (l.2309-2312): "To determine damage to the target inflicted by a death from above attack, divide the weight of the attacking ’Mech by 10 and multiply the result by 3... a 55-ton Griffin inflicts 17 points of damage." (Weight/5 is the *attacker's self-damage*, l.2243: "If a DFA hits, divide the attacker’s weight by 5".)
   - "Per leg" appears nowhere in the source. A 55-ton 'Mech does 17 damage to the target, not 11 per leg (22).

5. **Wrong number — standing up costs 2 MP, not 1.**
   - Summary (Prone 'Mechs): "Standing up costs 1 MP and requires a Piloting Skill Roll."
   - Source (l.1014-1015): "Each attempt to stand, successful or not, costs 2 MP. The ’Mech’s pilot must make a Piloting Skill Roll"; Movement Costs Table (l.903-904): "Standing up — 2/attempt." (1 MP is the cost of *dropping* prone, l.1091.)

### Minor findings

- **Punch/kick damage phrased as "Arm weight / 10" and "Leg weight / 5".** Source bases both on the *attacking 'Mech's total weight* (l.2332-2333: punch = "1 for every 10 tons... that the attacker weighs"; l.2368-2369: kick = "1 point for every 5 tons of the attacking ’Mech’s weight"). The divisors are right; "arm/leg weight" is wrong as written.
- **Heat table compression misstates onset of to-hit modifiers.** Summary: "5-8 | Movement penalties, to-hit modifiers." Source: movement penalties begin at 5 heat (l.3253), but weapon to-hit modifiers begin at 8 (l.3272: "At 8, 13, 17, and 24 heat points"). At heat 5-7 there is no to-hit modifier.
- **Target movement modifier understated at high speed.** Summary: "+0 (stood still) to +4+ (moved 18+ hexes)." Source Attack Modifiers Table (l.1636-1639): moved 18-24 hexes = +5, 25+ = +6 (+4 corresponds to 10-17 hexes).
- **Minimum movement omits the running requirement.** Summary: "A 'Mech can always move 1 hex forward regardless of terrain cost." Source (l.918, l.734-735): "To enter a hex in this fashion, a ’Mech must use running movement," and it must be the only MP expended that turn.
- **Omission: club and physical-weapon (hatchet) attacks.** Source lists seven physical attack types (l.1938-1941); the summary lists only punch, kick, charge, DFA, and push. Club attacks (1 damage per 5 tons, l.2145-2146) and hatchets are a full subsystem.
- **Omission: pilot death at six wounds as a destruction condition.** Source (l.3011-3013): destroyed "if six MechWarrior wounds are taken." Summary's Scoring section lists only CT destruction, head destruction, 3 engine hits, and ammo explosion ("pilot incapacitation" is mentioned only loosely in End Phase).
- **Components are generic.** Source box contents (l.323-364): 8 plastic miniatures, 15 pre-filled record sheets + 1 blank, 2 mapsheets, 2 dice, MechWarrior cards, punchboard, etc. The summary's generic list ("miniatures or standees," "various reference tables") isn't wrong, but carries no counts. Setup's "agreed Battle Value" force selection is not described in the source setup rules (BV appears only as a stat on a sample record sheet, l.535).

### Supported mechanics (spot-checked)

Shutdown thresholds (summary "14+ possible, 30 automatic" vs source 14/18/22/26 with Avoid rolls, no avoid at 30, l.3279-3284) — acceptable compression. Heat 0-4 no effect (first effect at 5, l.3253). Victory = last surviving side (l.690-691). Destruction via CT structure loss (l.2899-2900) and head destruction (l.2946-2947). Hit location 2d6 with front/left/right/rear columns (l.1839-1841, 1910-1912); armor then internal structure (l.2482-2488). Each weapon fires once per turn (l.1306). 2d6 >= modified TN hits (l.1268-1270). Water: D1 partial cover (l.1045-1046), D2+ submerged (l.1047-1048). PSR triggers for losing a leg (l.2950), being kicked (l.2372), water entry (l.1041), rough/rubble (l.811-813, 449).
