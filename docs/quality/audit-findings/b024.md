### Findings
1. **(Major) Aircraft carrier cost wrong:** summary 16 vs source 14 (l.1636). Appears in both the unit table and Player Reference key costs.
2. **(Major) AA gun cost wrong:** summary 5 vs source 6 (l.1506). Appears in both unit table and key costs.
3. **(Major) Strategic bombing damage cap wrong:** summary "max damage = territory IPC value"; source caps total damage at **twice** the territory's IPC value (l.1539, 1610-1611). Halves the effectiveness of bombing if played as written.

7. (Minor) "Amphibious Assaults ... Defenders fire AA before landing" is muddled — AA fire targets attacking *air units* only, never landing troops.

Verified clean: turn orders, scenario VC counts, 18-city list (l.514-532 incl. ### Findings
1. **(Major) Stacking limit wrong:** summary states "4 combat units per hex" with Trucks/Supply excluded — repeated three times (Actions, Special Rules, Player Reference). Source: 12 units per hex, Trucks count as units, Supply tokens count as 1 unit collectively, plus per-type caps of 6 Infantry / 6 Tanks / 3 Artillery. This is a core constraint on every move, retreat, and reinforcement; playing with a 4-unit cap drastically changes the game.
2. **(Minor) "Ongoing Campaign" optional rule mischaracterized:** summary says "Continue playing beyond turn 8"; source says keep records of scores across multiple games, reverse roles, and compare tallies (l.756-758). No play beyond turn 8 exists.
3. **(Minor omission) Supply income:** each turn Axis receives 9 and Allies 11 Supply tokens (l.695-697). The summary never says how supplies are replenished, despite supply being the fuel for all attacks/moves.
4. (Minor omissions) Commandeering enemy Trucks/Supply tokens (l.653-666), asymmetric front-line updating (Axis claims the 3 eastern hexes behind an occupied hex; Allies claim only the occupied hex, l.741-745), ground-combat initiative roll each turn after the first (l.438-440), and direct edge-placement of reinforcements (non-Truck units can be placed on board-edge hexes directly, not only "via Trucks", l.698-714).
5. Verified clean: surprise attack (Allies don't attack turn 1, l.437, 442), river barriers Infantry-only incl. retreats (l.173-176, 537), blitz mechanics (l.267-271), retreat restrictions and destroyed-if-unable (l.533-540), Truck capacity 6 (l.329), free-Truck return (l.340-341, 577-590), air combat sequence (l.360-404), attacked units don't fire back (consistent: summary never claims defenders fire).

**Verdict: MAJOR** — core stacking rule contradicted (4 vs 12 with 6/6/3 type caps), plus an invented "play beyond turn 8" option and omitted supply income.
