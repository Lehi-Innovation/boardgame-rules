## horizon-zero-dawn-the-board-game

**Verdict: MAJOR**

### Finding 1 — Wrong Win Condition (victory currency)

**Summary:** "After the final encounter, glory is tallied. The hunter with the most total glory wins, earning the title of Hawk."

**Source (lines 1832–1835):** "Once glory has been tallied, Sun tokens have been distributed, and the Leader and Fledgling tokens have been passed to new players (if required), all glory points are discarded."

**Source (lines 2132–2139):** "Each player then totals how many victory points they have based on their Sun tokens. A Blazing Sun is worth 3 points, a Full Sun is worth 2 points, and a Half Sun is worth 1 point. Although all players have won and can hold their heads proudly aloft at a successful hunt, the player with the most victory points has proven themselves the first among equals and emerges as the winner of the game."

**Finding:** The summary claims the winner is the player with "the most total glory" at game end. This is completely wrong. Glory is earned per-encounter, used only to award Sun tokens at each Campfire's Victory Step, then entirely discarded. The actual win condition at the Hunters' Call is the highest SUM OF SUN TOKEN VALUES (Blazing Sun=3, Full Sun=2, Half Sun=1). A player tracking "glory" across the game and using it to determine the winner would produce a wrong result.

### Finding 2 — Tiebreaker wrong

**Summary:** "Tied rankings are broken by specific rules." (vague)

**Source (lines 2144–2148):** "If there's a tie, the player who currently has possession of the Leader token wins, if they are one of the tied players. If there's still a tie, or neither tied player has the Leader token, both players are crowned as victorious."

**Finding:** The summary gives no detail on the tiebreaker (Leader token). While the summary's vagueness does not state a wrong rule, the complete omission of this critical rule (used when players tie on Sun token VP) means a table playing with the summary would not know how to resolve ties. Combined with the wrong win currency, this compounds the error.

### Finding 3 — End Game Trigger: Cooperative failure not described

**Summary describes the game end only as:** "After the final encounter, glory is tallied."

**Source (lines 2123–2131):** "If the players are unsuccessful and don't complete the encounter, they collectively lose the game ... If the players manage to successfully complete the encounter, their quest is over. Immediately resolve the victory step of the Campfire Phase..."

**Finding:** The summary omits the cooperative failure condition entirely (all players lose if the final Hunters' Call encounter is failed). This is a MAJOR omission since the summary could lead players to believe the game ends only with individual ranking.

The vast majority of the source content is blank lines, control characters (soft-hyphens, special chars), and section headers only. The few readable fragments are: section headers ("PHASE – WAY OF LIFE", "PHASE – ACTIONS", "END OF THE GAME AND SCORING", "SHOP ACTIONS", "APPENDIX 1-3", fire months "5, 8 and 11"), component token labels (Pipes x8, Tobacco pouches x8, Fish x8), and card group labels (Spring x14, Summer x14, Autumn x14, Winter x14).
