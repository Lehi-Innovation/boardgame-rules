### Finding 1 (MAJOR): Victory Point computation entirely missing

Summary (Scoring): "**Victory Points** come from each pirate's **Notoriety Points** and **Net Worth**" and "Retirement: ... earning bonus VP." That is all the summary says about converting NP/Net Worth into VP.

Source 18.12 / 18.3 specify the actual scheme, none of which appears in the summary:
- Retired pirate: "each accumulated Notoriety Point earns **two VPs** ... every **100 Doubloons** of his accumulated Net Worth earns **one VP**".
- Eliminated or unretired at game end: "one VP for each of the Pirate's accumulated Notoriety Points but **zero VP from his accumulated Net Worth**" (Net Worth is lost).
- KC eliminates a pirate: KC's player gains "one VP for every two of the Pirate's accumulated Notoriety Points (with remainders dropped)" — this VP source is absent from the summary entirely.

Impact: a table playing from the summary cannot score the game, and the unconditional phrasing ("VP come from ... Net Worth") actively misleads — players would count Net Worth for unretired/eliminated pirates and miss the 2× NP retirement multiplier. This is the game's win condition, so it is a major scoring omission.

### Finding 2 (minor error): D&R restriction overstated

Summary: "Pirates in D&R cannot act until a D&R Recovery Action is spent."
Source 13.22: "The only two Actions available to a Pirate bearing a D&R marker are **Movement** ... and D&R Recovery." Also 13.24: Voluntary D&R needs one Recovery Action; **Involuntary D&R needs two consecutive** Recovery Actions. The summary forbids movement that is actually allowed and misses the 1-vs-2 recovery distinction.

### Finding 3 (minor error): Map region misnamed

Summary (Components): board shows "the North Atlantic, West Africa, and East Africa/India regions."
Source 2.2: "three major Regions—**the Americas**, West Africa, and East Africa/India." (The North Atlantic is one of the ten Sea Areas, not a Region.) Cosmetic, not gameplay-relevant.

### Finding 4 (minor omissions)

- Retirement methods 18.41C (free retirement in own Safe Haven), 18.41D (bribe Pro-Pirate Governor, D66 × 100 from Net Worth), 18.41B (surrender to KC with Letter of Marque), and 18.41E (General Pardon forced retirement on its 2nd play) are not mentioned.
- Defeat of a KC in battle earns 2× the KC's Combat rating in NP (10.35, 18.2); summary's notoriety list omits KCs (it lists only warships at 1×).
- Automatic mutiny when Crew Loyalty reaches 0 (14.1) omitted; summary attributes mutiny only to the Mutiny Conspiracy card.
- 17.14: at least one "Hold Until Played" card must be played each Player-Turn — omitted.
- KC deploy restriction "not In-Port or in a Transit Box" (10.12) omitted.

None of these minor items would alone exceed MINOR; the verdict is driven by Finding 1.

**Verdict: MAJOR** — all spot-checked numbers are accurate and no mechanics are invented, but the VP conversion rules (2 VP/NP retired vs 1 VP/NP otherwise; Net Worth scores only on retirement at 1 VP/100 Doubloons; KC half-NP award) are missing, leaving the game unscoreable from the summary and the unconditional Net-Worth claim misleading.
