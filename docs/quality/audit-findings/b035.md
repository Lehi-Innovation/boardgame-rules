### Findings

1. **MINOR — Omitted tournament tiebreak rule.** Summary (Scoreboard Phase): "Ties at highlights: ball carrier wins; if ball at midfield, it's a draw." Source adds a distinct rule for tournaments: "In case of a tie at a tournament when the ball is at midfield, the first manager decides which tied team has higher Star Power" (l.653-654). The summary covers the highlight case correctly but omits the tournament case entirely.
2. **SUSPECT/UNVERIFIABLE — Piling On trigger.** Summary: "Piling On: On Tackler Down result, may attempt another tackle against a different player." Source: "Piling On: Each time you roll [icon(s) lost in extraction] during this player's tackle attempt, this player may attempt another tackle against a different opposing player" (l.922-925). The trigger icon did not survive extraction, so the "Tackler Down" claim cannot be confirmed. It is suspect: a Tackler Down trigger would conflict with the downed-player rule ("Downed players lose all abilities and remaining skills," l.571-572), suggesting the trigger is more likely the Target Down result. Not counted as a confirmed error due to OCR loss; flagged for re-check against the PDF.
3. **Trivial — Named abilities list is a subset.** Summary lists 8 of the 15 named abilities; Dauntless, Dirty Player, Dump-Off, Fend, Frenzy, Juggernaut, Strip Ball, and the Morg 'N Thorg duplicate rule (l.830-833) are omitted. These are card-text abilities reprinted on the cards themselves, so omission is unlikely to mislead.
4. **Trivial — Other omissions.** Either/Or central payouts (l.769-773); moved-vs-committed player rule (moved players don't reuse skills, l.804-814); 2-manager "alone at tournament collects winner + loser payouts" (l.850-852); optional variants (Abbreviated/Extended Season, Scheduling Limitations). None misleading; variants are reasonable to omit.

### Findings

1. **MINOR (clear error) — Component figure counts wrong.** Summary Components: "40 Warrior figures (10 per clan), 4 Leader figures (1 per clan), 4 Ship figures (1 per clan)" — 48 clan figures total. Source: "10 Bear Clan Figures / 10 Wolf / 10 Serpent / 10 Raven" (l.122-126), i.e., 40 clan figures total, each clan's 10 comprising 1 Leader + 8 Warriors + 1 Ship per the setup text: "Take your clan's Leader figure and 8 Warrior figures... Take one Ship figure" (l.267-270). Warriors are 8 per clan (32 total), not 10 per clan (40). The summary's own Setup section correctly says "Take Leader + 8 Warriors... and 1 Ship," so the gameplay-relevant number is right and the Components entry is internally contradicted. Unlikely to mislead at the table, but it is a wrong number.
2. **Trivial (extrapolation) — Quest glory range.** Summary says quests give "5-10+ Glory each"; the source only evidences 5 and 7 Glory in examples (l.593, l.810). Not contradicted, but the range is not sourced.
3. **Trivial (inference) — "final (6th) step."** Source says only "the last step" (l.874); the summary labels it the 6th. Plausible (consistent with 4th/5th-step wording implying a 6-step track) but not explicit in the text. Not contradicted.
4. **Trivial (structure) — End of an Age folded into Release Valhalla.** Summary places "flip Pillage tokens, pass First Player token" inside phase 6; source presents these as a separate "End of an Age" step after phase 6 (l.837-844). Same effect and timing; no gameplay impact.
5. **Trivial omissions.** Region names (Manheim, Alfheim, Jotunheim, l.140-141); provinces have 3-5 villages, 1 figure per village (l.144-147); each deck contains eight 4+ and six 3+ cards (l.338); destroyed-figures-still-can-win-via-battle-card edge case (l.651-656); first-game no-draft suggestion. None misleading — the village/occupancy concept is conveyed implicitly via "empty villages" throughout.
