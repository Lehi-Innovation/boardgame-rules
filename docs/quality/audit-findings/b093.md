# Audit Findings — Batch b093

## fields-of-arle

**Verdict: MAJOR**

---

### Finding 1 — Feeding restricted to winter only (MAJOR)

**Summary claims (Special Rules):**
> "Feeding: Workers must be fed during winter inventorying; insufficient food costs points."

**Source contradicts this:** Sustenance is required at the end of EVERY half-year (both summer and winter), not only winter.

- November (end of Summer/Fall half-year): "4. Sustenance: Pay 3 Food and 2 Peat (as fuel for the upcoming Winter)." ... "If you are still missing Food ... or Fuel ... you lose 2 Victory Points per missing item."
- May (end of Winter/Spring half-year): "4. Sustenance: Pay 3 Food." ... "If you are still missing Food, you lose 2 Victory Points per missing Food."

Also confirmed by keyword index: "Food: ... for Sustenance each Half Year ..."

The summary says feeding occurs only "during winter inventorying," which is wrong in two ways:
1. Feeding happens every half-year (5 summer + 4 winter = 9 feeding events), not just 4 winter ones.
2. Summer inventorying also requires paying 2 Peat as fuel — this entire cost is absent from the summary.

A player following the summary would feed only in winter and never pay Peat for fuel, drastically misplaying the game.

---

### Finding 2 — Missing Large Vehicle penalty in scoring (MINOR)

**Summary claims (Scoring / Victory Conditions):**
> "Vehicles" listed generically as a scoring source with no further detail.

**Source states (scoring section):**
> "Add up the VP from your Plows, Peat Boats, and Vehicles. Then, subtract 3 VP unless you have at least one Large Vehicle in your Barn."

The -3 VP penalty for lacking a Large Vehicle is a notable scoring rule entirely absent from the summary. However, since the scoring section is presented as a high-level list without specific vehicle VP values, this is classified MINOR (the major finding alone controls the verdict).
