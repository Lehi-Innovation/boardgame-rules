### Finding 1 (MAJOR — invented mechanic): "Sail" action strip does not exist
- Summary (Actions): "**Sail:** Additional ship movement or boat manipulation."
- Source: the 7 action strips are Pioneer, Settle, Cattle-breeding, Region Scoring, Hire, Builder, and Progress (each named in the Actions section of the rules). There is no Sail action; "sailing" appears only in the flavor intro. Ship movement is Phase B of every turn, not an action strip.

### Finding 2 (MAJOR — wrong mechanic): "Modernize" misdescribed
- Summary: "**Modernize:** Build or upgrade production sites on your player board."
- Source: "Choose one of the unoccupied spaces in the modernization area of your player board. Then pay the coins indicated in order to take 1 lever tile from the supply and put it in the recess." Modernizing buys lever tiles; production sites are built via other effects (river reward, Explore reward, card effects). A player following the summary would use the wrong action for production sites and never learn how levers are acquired.

### Finding 3 (MAJOR — wrong mechanic): "Build/Builder" misdescribed
- Summary: "**Build:** Place buildings (houses/settlements) with specific bonuses."
- Source (Builder action): "you get 3 coins at the beginning of the action (instead of playing 1 card, or discarding it to get 2 coins). Then you may either play 1 card of any kind or draw 2 cards." Builder is a coins/cards action; it places no buildings. Buildings are placed via Develop/Upgrade effects (Settle and Pioneer strips).
- Related error in Turn Structure: "discard a card for 2 coins (3 coins with Builder)" — the source gives 3 coins outright with Builder *instead of* the play/discard option, not an improved discard rate.

### Finding 4 (MAJOR — omitted action): Region Scoring strip missing entirely
- Source: "Then you choose one of the 4 regions... Put a region marker on the reward... and gain the reward... After that, the other 3 regions are scored for all players (yourself included). Everybody gets 1 coin for each of their presences... in these 3 regions."
- The summary never mentions this action — one of the 7 strips and a core income mechanic for all players. The Pioneer strip (explore twice + its only-other-players right-side exception) is also absent.

### Finding 5 (MAJOR — contradiction in final scoring): summary invents VP for leftover coins/resources
- Summary (Final Scoring): "VP from remaining resources, coins, and other bonuses."
- Source: "Leftover coins and cards don't get you any victory points." (Resources are virtual and never held.) The summary also omits the actual final-scoring sources: 2 VP per lever with 4-6 levers (3 VP each with 7+), and 2/4/6 VP per vacated recess in tiers 3/4/5 of the player board.

### Finding 6 (MAJOR — omission): interim scoring procedure gutted
- Summary describes interim scoring only as "Scoring criteria are determined by the 4 scoring tiles."
- Source: interim scoring is a 5-step procedure: (1) play 1-2 cards or upgrade; (2) **mandatory** scoring for one tile per player, with the 1/2/3/4-VP scoring marker — and if the condition is not met you **subtract** the points, doubled (plus or minus) on your chosen tile; (3) income phase (income tracks + vacated player-board spaces + 1 VP per settlement adjacent to each of your cattle); (4) reset levers, 1 VP per unused lever; (5) clear region markers. The summary omits the minus-points risk, the entire income engine, and the lever reset/bonus. It also omits that the 4th interim scoring waits until all players have had equal turns and pays out VP only (no coins/cards).

### Finding 7 (MINOR — wrong mechanic): "Settle" misdescribed
- Summary: "**Settle:** Place a house on a building tile, then optionally upgrade to a settlement."
- Source: the Develop effect places an *inhabitant* from your ranch on a building site; Upgrade converts inhabitant→house (give up 1 inhabitant) or house→settlement (give up 2 inhabitants, requires 3 adjacent presences, extra cost of 1/2 inhabitants or 4 VP each for the 3rd/4th settlement, and -5 VP for a second settlement in the same region). All of these costs/conditions are omitted.

### Finding 8 (MINOR — omissions)
- Cattle-breeding costs omitted: give up 1/2/3/4 inhabitants for a pasture holding 0/1/2/3 cattle (max 4 cattle per pasture), extra cost for your 4th/5th cattle, +2 coins per adjacent house.
- Hire cost omitted: pay 3 coins or discard 3 cards per inhabitant (all players).
- River fork rule omitted (upper arm in round 1, lower arm in round 2).
- Tie rule omitted ("In case of a tie, all the tied players win").
- "Lever effects... provide one-time bonuses" — levers reset and are reusable at each interim scoring.

**Verdict: MAJOR** — component and setup numbers are accurate, but the Actions and Scoring sections contain an invented action (Sail), three misdescribed actions (Modernize, Builder, Settle), a missing action (Region Scoring), a final-scoring claim directly contradicted by the source, and the interim-scoring/income engine — the heart of the game — is essentially absent.
