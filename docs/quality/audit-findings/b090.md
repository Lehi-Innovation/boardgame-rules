## falling-sky-the-gallic-revolt-against-caesar

**Verdict: MAJOR**

### Finding 1 — Roman victory condition wrong (missing "Subdued + Dispersed" formula, invented "Control + Senate-track threshold Regions")

Summary: "Rome: Control + Allied Tribes in Senate-track threshold Regions"

Source (§7.2): "Romans: Subdued plus Dispersed plus Roman Allied Tribes exceeds 15."

The summary introduces "Control" (which is NOT part of the Roman victory formula) and replaces the correct description with "Senate-track threshold Regions," which is not how the Roman victory condition works. The Senate track governs Legion reinforcements (§6.5), not the victory threshold. The actual condition is a simple numeric threshold of 15 combining Subdued + Dispersed + Roman Allied Tribes.

### Finding 2 — Arverni victory condition: Off-map Legions condition entirely omitted

Summary: "Arverni: Allied Tribes + Citadels exceed a threshold"

Source (§7.2): "Arverni: Off-map Legions (Fallen, plus on the Legions Track, plus removed by Event) exceed six AND Arverni Allied Tribes plus Arverni Citadels exceeds eight."

Source (§7.2 note): "The Arverni, unlike the other Factions, must fulfill both of two separate conditions."

The summary omits the Off-map Legions condition entirely. The Arverni are the only faction requiring TWO simultaneous conditions. A player following the summary could believe Arverni win simply by accumulating Allied Tribes + Citadels over a threshold, but in fact they also need to knock at least 7 Legions off the map, which is a completely separate strategic requirement.

### Finding 3 — Aedui victory condition wrong (invented "controlled Regions")

Summary: "Aedui: Allied Tribes + number of specific controlled Regions"

Source (§7.2): "Aedui: Aedui Allied Tribes plus Citadels exceeds those of each other Faction (Gallic, Germanic, and Roman; do not count Forts)."

The summary invents a "controlled Regions" element that does not appear anywhere in the Aedui victory condition. The actual condition is comparative (must exceed every other faction's total), not threshold-based, and involves no Regions.

### Finding 4 — Belgae victory condition (minor issue)

Summary: "Belgae: Control of Belgica Regions + Allies meets threshold"

Source (§7.2): "Belgae: Total Belgic Control Value (Tribe spaces under Belgic Control, regardless of Allies, and neither Dispersed nor Suebi) plus Belgic Allies and Citadels exceeds 15."

The summary says "Belgica Regions" but the source says all Regions under Belgic Control (including Celtica), not just Belgica. The threshold of 15 is also absent from the summary. This adds to the MAJOR verdict.

### Finding 5 — Tiebreaker is specific but summary is vague

Summary: "If still no winner, each Faction's distance from victory is compared."

Source (§7.1): "Ties go to any Non-players (8.0), then the Romans, then the Arverni, then the Aedui."

The source has a specific tiebreaker order (Non-players > Romans > Arverni > Aedui). The summary vaguely says "distance from victory is compared" (which is the margin calculation for determining 1st/2nd/3rd place, §7.3), but omits the specific faction priority order for breaking ties.

### Numbers checked

NUMBERS_CHECKED: 5 (faction victory thresholds: Roman 15, Arverni 6 Legions + 8 Allies/Citadels, Belgic 15; card deck 77; factions acting per card up to 2), SUPPORTED: 3 (77 cards, 2 factions per card, Belgic 15 with caveat above)
