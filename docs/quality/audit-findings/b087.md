## ethnos

**Verdict: MAJOR**

### Finding 1 — Trolls mechanic completely wrong (MAJOR)
**Summary:** "Trolls: Take a Troll token (worth 2 Glory at game end) if your Band size meets or exceeds the current Age threshold (Age I: 4+, Age II: 5+, Age III: 6+)."

**Source (lines 570-574):** "Troll tokens have no value in themselves, but they are used to break ties for control of the Kingdoms at the end of the Age. The tied player with the highest total value of Troll tokens wins the tie."

The summary fabricates: (a) that Troll tokens are "worth 2 Glory at game end" — source says they have NO value; (b) Age-threshold thresholds of 4+/5+/6+ — the source says you take any unclaimed Troll token with a value UP TO the number of cards in the Band you just played, no Age-specific thresholds; (c) the actual function (Kingdom tie-breaker) is completely omitted.

### Finding 2 — Minotaurs ability is wrong (Wingfolk's ability assigned to Minotaurs) (MAJOR)
**Summary:** "Minotaurs: Place a Control marker in any Kingdom (not just the Leader's color)."

**Source (lines 506-508):** "If you choose a Minotaur to be the Leader of a Band of Allies, you need one less card than normal to place your Control marker in the matching Kingdom."

The "any Kingdom" ability belongs to Wingfolk (source line 590-591): "If your Band of Allies is led by a Wingfolk Leader, you may place your Control marker in any Kingdom on the game board." The summary correctly describes Wingfolk's ability under Wingfolk, but also incorrectly assigns it to Minotaurs while omitting Minotaurs' actual "one less card" ability entirely.

### Finding 3 — Skeletons scoring description is wrong (MAJOR)
**Summary:** "Skeletons: Every Skeleton card in any Band (not just the Leader's) counts as +1 to Band size for Glory scoring. As Leader: no special ability beyond this."

**Source (lines 547-558):** "Unlike other Tribes, Skeletons can never be the Leader of a Band of Allies...at the end of every Age, all Skeletons, wherever they are, must be discarded before awarding Glory for the Bands of Allies. For example, if you have a Band of 5 Allies that includes 2 Skeletons, at the end of the Age those Skeletons are discarded, and you only score Glory for the 3 cards left behind."

Skeletons are removed BEFORE scoring, reducing band size — not adding +1. The summary inverts this entirely. Players using the summary would score Skeleton bands incorrectly.

### Other claims verified
- Band glory table: source example "1 Glory for the Band with 2 green cards, 6 Glory for the Band with 3 Dwarves, and 6 Glory for the Band of 4 red cards" — consistent with the summary table (2=1, 3=3... wait, 3 Dwarves gives 6 which matches the Dwarves bonus of +1 extra card, so 3+1=4 cards = 6 glory). Table values in summary (1=0, 2=1, 3=3, 4=6, 5=10, 6+=15) appear supported by example and context.
- Centaurs: play another Band if you place a marker. Source matches.
- Dwarves: Band scores as if one extra card. Source matches.
- Elves: Keep cards equal to Band size. Source matches.
- Giants: 2 Glory immediately + Age-end bonus. Source matches (Age I: 2, Age II/III values on token — confirmed for Age I).
- Halflings: Cannot place Control markers; 24-card deck. Source matches.
- Merfolk: Advance by Band size; pass marker symbol = extra Control marker; scores like 7th Kingdom. Source matches.
- Orcs: Place marker on Orc Horde board; scoring per board. Source says 6 Glory for 3 markers (example). Summary says "1/3/6/10/15 for 1-5 markers" — not directly verified from OCR'd table but consistent with Orc board reference.
- Wingfolk: Place in any Kingdom. Source matches.
- Wizards: Draw cards from deck equal to Band size. Source matches.
- Game end tiebreakers: most Control markers, then largest Band. Source matches.
- 2-player Band rule: more cards than total (both players') markers in Kingdom. Source matches.

NUMBERS_CHECKED: 15, SUPPORTED: 12

## evergreen

**Verdict: MAJOR**

### Finding 1 — Fertility scoring wrong: "planted spaces" vs. "Big Trees only" (MAJOR)
**Summary (Scoring section):** "Fertility: For each Biome, count the Fertility icons in the Fertility Zone. Multiply by the number of planted spaces you have in that Biome on your planet."

**Source (lines 488-497):** "The number of points you get from each of your Biomes depends on its Fertility value and the number of Big Trees it contains...You get points equal to the Fertility value of the Biome for each Big Tree that is in that Biome."

The summary says "planted spaces" (which would include Sprouts and Small Trees); the source specifies only **Big Trees** count. This fundamentally changes Fertility strategy and final score calculation.

### Finding 2 — Invented Lake end-game scoring (MAJOR)
**Summary (End-Game Scoring):** "Lakes: Each Lake scores points equal to the number of Trees adjacent to it."

**Source:** No such end-game Lake scoring appears anywhere in the source. The only Lake rule is a Power effect: "Place Lake: Place 1 Lake pawn from the pool into an empty space of your Planet board. Immediately apply 1 Generic Growth effect on up to 2 different spaces orthogonally adjacent to that Lake." (The source also states "Sprouts, Bushes, and Lakes do not collect Light.") No end-game Lake scoring exists. Searched: "Lake.*point", "Lake.*score", "Lake.*adjacent.*point", "end.*Lake", "final.*Lake" — no matches.

### Finding 3 — Wrong tiebreaker (MAJOR)
**Summary:** "Tiebreaker: most Big Trees on planet."

**Source (lines 503-506):** "In case of a tie, the winner is the tied player who comes first in turn order, based on the final position of the First Player token after the last round."

The tiebreaker is turn order position determined by the First Player token, not most Big Trees.

### Other claims verified

- Draft pool: players+1. Source matches.
- Unchosen card with Fertility icon → Fertility Zone; no icon → discard. Source matches.
- Light scoring: Small Trees = 1 point, Big Trees = 2 points when hit by Light. Source matches.
- Largest Forest: points equal to number of connected Trees+Bushes in largest group. Source matches (source says "1 point for each Small Tree, Big Tree, and/or Bush that is part of your Biggest Forest").
- Sun rotates clockwise after each Season scoring. Source matches.
- Deck reshuffle if empty (not including Fertility Zone cards). Source matches.
- Starting points by turn order: Source OCR shows "3Pt." at position 4 of the turn order but full table not readable. Summary claims 1st=0, 2nd=1, 3rd=2, 4th=3. Cannot fully verify due to OCR.
